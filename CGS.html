Blue-Green Space Opportunities

Natural river management opportunities

Floodplain woodlands: slow water, reduce, delay peak
Catchment woodlands: slow, store water, reduce peak and frequency
Leaky barrier: slow velocity, peak, store. natural interception of river, increases connectivity
Offline Storage area: temporary floodplain to reduce peak
River Restoration: reduce peak, velocity. restores physical process
